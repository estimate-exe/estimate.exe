Hey there,

On this page, you'll discover a user-friendly GPA calculator that allows you to determine your GPA by entering your earned grades and corresponding credits.

Simply input the credit hours for each course and the grades you received, and the calculator will instantly compute your resulting GPA.

If you want to calculate your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average), you can click here.

If you wish to calculate the required GPA to achieve a targeted CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average), you can click here.

Feel free to use the help button provided if you need any assistance.

I suggest you to go through the guide for a better understanding of this tool.
