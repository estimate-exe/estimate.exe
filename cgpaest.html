On this page, you'll discover a CGPA calculator that allows you to determine your CGPA by entering your earned grades and corresponding credits of the current semester along with the Previous CGPA and Previous Total Credits.

And further down the page you'll discover another CGPA estimator which will estimate the Overall CGPA by entering the current semester expected GPA and credits along with the Previous CGPA and Previous Total Credits.

If you want to calculate only the current semester GPA, you can click here.

If you wish to calculate the required GPA to achieve a targeted CGPA, you can click here.

Feel free to use the help button provided if you need any assistance.

I suggest you go through the guide for a better understanding of this tool.

CGPA Calculator with Grades & Credits.

Need Help?

Inputs:

Semester: It is the number of the semester for your reference which is completely optional.
Credits: These are the credit weights of each course/subject for the current semester.
Grades: These are the grades obtained for each course/subject for the current semester.

Previous CGPA: It is the CGPA you scored upto the previous semester (before this semester).
Total Credits: It is the total number of credits before the current semester (upto the previous semester). DO NOT inculde the credits of the current semester.

Click on the 'Interchange Columns' button to swap the positions of the columns.

Click on the 'Add another course' button to add a course.

Click on 'Calculate CGPA' to calculate the result (CGPA).

Output:

You will get the resultant GPA of the current semester.
CGPA upto the current semester.
And Total Credits upto the current semester.

Tip: If you want to estimate the CGPA of the next semester you can repeat this whole process but with inputs as,
Estimated or expected grades and credits of the next semester.
Previous CGPA: The CGPA you calculated before.
Total Credits: Total credits which has been displayed in the previous result.

Feel free to refer the guide for a better understanding.
